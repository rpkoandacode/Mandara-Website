第二课: 留串钥匙给父母

串

chuàn – bunch string

周末我喜欢去朋友家串门。

On weekends, I like visiting friends’ homes.

他买了一串烤肉边走边吃。

He bought a skewer of grilled meat and ate it while walking.

一辈子

yí bèizi – a lifetime

我希望能和家人幸福地生活一辈子。

I hope to live happily with my family for a lifetime.

他一辈子都在努力工作。

He worked hard his whole life.

农村

nóngcūn – countryside

他小时候在农村长大。

He grew up in the countryside.

现在很多年轻人从农村搬到城市生活。

Nowadays, many young people move from rural areas to cities.

屋子

wūzi – room / house

这个屋子很干净，也很安静。

This room is very clean and quiet.

他一个人住在小小的屋子里。

He lives alone in a small room.

断

duàn – to break

突然停电了，网络也断了。

Suddenly the power went out, and the internet was cut off.

他不小心把绳子弄断了。

He accidentally broke the rope.

姥姥

lǎolao – maternal grandmother

我小时候经常和姥姥一起生活。

When I was young, I often lived with my grandma.

姥姥做的菜特别好吃。

My grandma’s cooking is especially delicious.

舅舅

jiùjiu – maternal uncle

我舅舅在北京工作。

My uncle works in Beijing.

周末我去舅舅家做客。

I visit my uncle’s home on weekends.

姑姑

gūgu – paternal aunt

我姑姑对我很好，经常给我买礼物。

My aunt is very nice to me and often buys me gifts.

姑姑今天来我们家吃饭。

My aunt is coming to our house for dinner today.

坚决

jiānjué – firm / determined

他坚决不同意这个决定。

He firmly disagrees with this decision.

面对困难，我们要坚决坚持下去。

When facing difficulties, we must firmly persist.

打工

dǎgōng – to work a job

他在大城市打工已经三年了。

He has been working in a big city for three years.

很多学生暑假会去打工赚点钱。

Many students work during summer vacation to earn some money.

挣

zhèng – to earn

他每天努力工作，就是为了多挣点钱。

He works hard every day just to earn more money.

我想靠自己的能力挣钱。

I want to earn money through my own ability.

县

xiàn – county

他出生在一个小县城。

He was born in a small county town.

这个县的发展越来越快了。

This county is developing faster and faster.

套

tào – set

他在市中心买了一套房子。

He bought an apartment in the city center.

这套衣服很适合你。

This set of clothes suits you well.

项

xiàng – item

他完成了一项重要的任务。

这项活动吸引了很多学生参加。

This activity attracted many students to participate.
