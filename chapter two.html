第二课: 留串钥匙给父母

串

chuàn – bunch string

周末我喜欢去朋友家串门。

On weekends, I like visiting friends’ homes.

他买了一串烤肉边走边吃。

He bought a skewer of grilled meat and ate it while walking.

一辈子

yí bèizi – a lifetime

我希望能和家人幸福地生活一辈子。

I hope to live happily with my family for a lifetime.

他一辈子都在努力工作。

He worked hard his whole life.

农村

nóngcūn – countryside

他小时候在农村长大。

He grew up in the countryside.

现在很多年轻人从农村搬到城市生活。

Nowadays, many young people move from rural areas to cities.

屋子

wūzi – room / house

这个屋子很干净，也很安静。

This room is very clean and quiet.

他一个人住在小小的屋子里。

He lives alone in a small room.

断

duàn – to break

突然停电了，网络也断了。

Suddenly the power went out, and the internet was cut off.

他不小心把绳子弄断了。

He accidentally broke the rope.

姥姥

lǎolao – maternal grandmother

我小时候经常和姥姥一起生活。

When I was young, I often lived with my grandma.

姥姥做的菜特别好吃。

My grandma’s cooking is especially delicious.

舅舅

jiùjiu – maternal uncle

我舅舅在北京工作。

My uncle works in Beijing.

周末我去舅舅家做客。

I visit my uncle’s home on weekends.

姑姑

gūgu – paternal aunt

我姑姑对我很好，经常给我买礼物。

My aunt is very nice to me and often buys me gifts.

姑姑今天来我们家吃饭。

My aunt is coming to our house for dinner today.

坚决

jiānjué – firm / determined

他坚决不同意这个决定。

He firmly disagrees with this decision.

面对困难，我们要坚决坚持下去。

When facing difficulties, we must firmly persist.

打工

dǎgōng – to work a job

他在大城市打工已经三年了。

He has been working in a big city for three years.

很多学生暑假会去打工赚点钱。

Many students work during summer vacation to earn some money.

挣

zhèng – to earn

他每天努力工作，就是为了多挣点钱。

He works hard every day just to earn more money.

我想靠自己的能力挣钱。

I want to earn money through my own ability.

挣

他每天努力工作，就是为了多挣点钱。

He works hard every day just to earn more money

我想靠自己的能力挣钱。

I want to earn money through my own ability.

县

xiàn – county

他出生在一个小县城。

He was born in a small county town.

这个县的发展越来越快了。

This county is developing faster and faster.

套

tào – set

他在市中心买了一套房子。

He bought an apartment in the city center.

这套衣服很适合你。

This set of clothes suits you well.

装修

zhuāngxiū – to renovate

他们正在装修新房子。

They are renovating their new house.

这家餐厅装修得很漂亮。

This restaurant is beautifully decorated.

不得了

bù dé liǎo – extremely

今天热得不得了。

It’s extremely hot today.

他紧张得不得了，说话都不清楚了。

He was so nervous that he couldn’t even speak clearly.

以来

yǐlái – since

他来中国以来，汉语进步很快。

Since he came to China, his Chinese has improved quickly.

毕业以来，我一直在这家公司工作。

Since graduation, I have been working at this company.

醉

zuì – drunk

他喝了很多酒，已经醉了。

He drank a lot and is already drunk.

昨晚他喝醉了，走路都不稳。

Last night he got drunk and couldn’t even walk steadily.

强烈

qiángliè – strong

我对这个建议表示强烈支持。

I strongly support this suggestion.

阳光很强烈，记得戴帽子。

The sunlight is very strong， remember to wear a hat.

夜

yè – night

这座城市的夜很美。

The night in this city is very beautiful.

他常常工作到深夜。

He often works until late at night.

锁

suǒ – lock

出门前记得把门锁好。

Remember to lock the door before going out.

他把自行车锁在门口。

He locked his bicycle at the entrance.

临

lín – to face

考试临近了，我开始认真复习。

The exam is approaching, so I started reviewing seriously.

临走前，他和大家道别。

Before leaving, he said goodbye to everyone.

悄悄

qiāoqiāo – quietly / secretly

他悄悄地走进房间，没有打扰别人。

He quietly walked into the room without disturbing others.

她悄悄地告诉我一个秘密。

She quietly told me a secret.

晒

shài – to dry in the sun

今天阳光很好，我们把衣服拿去晒。

The sunlight is good today, so we take clothes to dry.

他在阳台上晒太阳。

He is sunbathing on the balcony.

被子

bèizi – quilt / blanket

冬天的时候，被子一定要盖厚一点。

In winter, the blanket should be thicker.

妈妈把被子拿出去晒了。

Mom took the quilt out to dry in the sun.

长途

chángtú – long-distance

我们坐了很久的长途汽车。

We took a long-distance bus for a long time.

长途旅行让人很累。

Long-distance travel makes people tired.

项

xiàng – item

他完成了一项重要的任务。

这项活动吸引了很多学生参加。

This activity attracted many students to participate.
