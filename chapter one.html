第一课: 了解生活

细节

xìjié - details

他做事情非常认真，总是注意每一个细节。

He is very serious about his work and always pays attention to every detail.

我们忽略一个重要细节。

We overlooked an important detail.

电台

diàntái - radio station

我每天早上都会听这个电台的新闻。

I listen to this radio station news every morning

我每天早上都会听这个电台的新闻。

The programs on this radio station are very interesting, and many people like them.

恩爱

ēn’ài - loving [husband and wife]

他们结婚很多年了，还是很恩爱。

They have been married for many years and are still very loving.

大家都很羡慕他们恩爱的生活。

Everyone envies their loving life.

对比

duìbǐ – to compare

把这两个方案对比一下，再做决定。

Compare these two plans first, then make a decision.

通过对比，我们发现这个方法更有效。

Through comparison, we found this method is more effective.

入围

rùwéi – to be shortlisted

他成功入围了这次比赛的决赛。

He successfully made it to the finals of this competition.

这部电影入围了多个国际奖项。

This movie was shortlisted for several international awards.

评委

píngwěi – judge

比赛结束后，评委们给出了评分。

After the competition, the judges gave their scores.

评委认为这位选手表现非常出色。

The judges think this contestant performed very well.

如何

rúhé – how

你能告诉我如何使用这个软件吗？

Can you tell me how to use this software?

老师问我们如何解决这个问题。

The teacher asked us how to solve this problem.

瘫痪

tānhuàn – paralysis

车祸导致他下半身瘫痪。

The car accident caused paralysis in his lower body.

系统瘫痪了，所有工作都暂停了。

The system was paralyzed, and all work was paused.

离婚

líhūn – divorce

他们因为性格不合而决定离婚。

They decided to divorce because of incompatible personalities.

离婚后，他搬到了另一个城市生活。

After the divorce, he moved to another city.

自杀

zìshā – suicide

警方正在调查他自杀的原因。

The police are investigating the reason for his suicide.

心理健康专家提醒大家要关注自杀预防。

Mental health experts remind everyone to pay attention to suicide prevention.

抱怨

bàoyuàn – to complain

他总是抱怨工作太累。

He always complains that work is too tiring

别总抱怨，多想办法解决问题。

Don’t always complain, try to think of solutions.

爱护

àihù – to take good care of

父母叫我们爱护环境。

Parents teach us to cherish the environment.

她非常爱护自己的宠物。

He takes very good care of his pet.

婚姻

hūnyīn – marriage

婚姻需要双方共同努力。

Marriage requires effort from both partners.

他们对婚姻有不同的看法。

They have different views about marriage.

吵架

chǎojià – to quarrel / argue

夫妻偶尔会吵架，但很快就和好了

Couples occasionally quarrel, but they quickly make up.

不要为了小事吵架这样很累。

Don't argue over small things, its exhausting.

相敬如宾

xiāng jìng rú bīn – treat each other with respect like guests.

他们结婚多年，一直相敬如宾。

They have been married for many years and always treat each other with respect.

在一段好的婚姻中，夫妻应该相敬如宾。

In a good marriage, couples should treat each other with mutual respect.

暗暗

àn’àn – secretly

她暗暗地下定决心，要更加努力。

He secretly made up his mind to work harder.

听到这个消息，他暗暗的高兴。

Hearing the news, she felt secretly happy

女士

nǚshì – lady

这位女士，请问您需要帮助吗？

Excuse me, ma'am, do you need help?

那位女士看起来很优雅。

That lady looks very elegant.

轮

lún – wheel

今天轮到我值班

Today is my turn to be on duty.

我们轮流做家务，这样比较公平。

We take turns doing housework, its more fair this way.

不耐烦

bù nàifán – impatient

他等了很久， 开始哟点不耐烦

He waited for a long time and started to get a bit impatient.

老师提醒我们不要对别人不耐烦

The teacher reminded us not to be impatient with others.

靠

kào – to lean on

她累了，就靠在墙上休息

He was tired, so he leaned against the wall to rest.

遇到困难的时候，我们要学会靠自己。

When facing difficulties, we should learn to rely on ourselves.

肩膀

jiānbǎng – shoulder

他轻轻的拍了拍我的肩膀。

He gently patted my shoulders.

难过的时候，你可以靠在我的肩膀上。

When you are sad, you can lean on my shoulder.

喊

hǎn – to shout

他在远处大声喊我的名字。

He shouted my name from far away.

老师喊我们赶快进教室。

The teacher called us to quickly enter the classroom.

伸

shēn – to stretch

他伸手拿桌子上的书。

He stretched out his hand to take the book on the table.

坐久了，可以站起来伸一伸身体。

After sitting for a long time, you can stand up and stretch your body.

手指

shǒuzhǐ – finger

他用手指指着地图上的位置。

He pointed at a place on the map with his finger.

冬天的时候，我的手指很容易变冷。

In winter, my fingers easily get cold.

递

dì – to hand

他把水递给了我。

He handed me the water.

请把文件递给老师

Please pass the document to the teacher.

脑袋

nǎodai – head

他拍了派自己的脑袋，好像想起了什么。

He patted his head as if he remembered something.

别低着脑袋，抬起头来。

Don't lower your head, lift it up.

叙述

xùshù – to narrate

他向我们叙述了是静的经过。

He describe to us what happened.

请用简单的话叙述你的想法。

Please describe your thoughts in simple words.

居然

jūrán – unexpectedly

他居然忘了今天的考试。

He unexpectedly forgot today's exam.

这么简单的问题，你居然不会？

Such a simple question, and you actually don't know it?

催

cuī – to urge

老师一直催我们按时交作业。

The teacher keeps urging us to submit homework on time.

我们已经在做了，你别再催我。

I'm already doing it, stop rushing me.

等待

děngdài – to wait

我在门口等待朋友的到来。

I waited at the door for my friend to arrive.

请耐心等待结果。

Please wait patiently for the result.

蚊子

wénzi – mosquito

夏天的蚊子特别多。

There are especially many mosquitoes in summer.

昨晚我被蚊子咬了好几次。

Last night i got bitten by mosquitoes several times.

半夜

bànyè – midnight

他常常半夜还在学习。

He often studies late at night.

半夜突然下起了大雨。

It suddenly started raining heavily at midnight.

叮

dīng – ding

手机叮的一声，收到了一条消息。

My phone made a "ding" sound and i received a message.

电梯到了，叮的一声门打开了。

The elevator arrived, and with a “ding,” the door opened.

老婆

lǎopo – wife

我老婆今天做了很好吃的晚饭。

他每天下班后都会陪老婆聊天。

He chats with his wife every day after work.

吵

chǎo – to quarrel

外面太吵了，我没办法专心学习。

It’s too noisy outside; I can’t focus on studying.

他们因为小事吵了起来。

They started arguing over a small matter.

项

xiàng – item

他完成了一项重要的任务。

这项活动吸引了很多学生参加。

This activity attracted many students to participate.
